## Findings

2. The RAF begins immediately and cannot be cancelled.
3. Play and New Game are interface transitions, not session transactions.
4. New Game does not reset resources, pressure, world, construction, roster, inventory, or active-session state.
5. Pause and Resume do not control gameplay ticking.
6. Title does not retire or stop the run.
7. Ended state persists after Outcome -> Title and can force Outcome again.
8. No session epoch, reset factory, stop, dispose, state fingerprint, or lifecycle result journal exists.
9. Movement and several secondary services remain implemented but unbound.
10. The existing smoke fixture cannot detect lifecycle or reset drift.
