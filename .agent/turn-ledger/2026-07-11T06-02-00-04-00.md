## Findings

1. `createOrchardGame()` constructs every mutable domain before the player starts a run.
2. Play, New Game, and Start are route transitions into the same `active-session` closure.
3. Pause, Title, and Outcome are also route transitions and do not gate domain ticks.
4. `kit-runtime.tick()` advances every domain on every RAF callback.
5. The RAF ID is not retained and there is no stop, restart, reset, or dispose service.
6. The delegated click listener has no removal handle.
7. `GameHost` exposes a raw engine plus unrestricted manual ticking without lifecycle admission.
8. An ended run remains ended. Selecting Title from Outcome is reversed by automatic Outcome routing on the next tick.
9. Starting again after an ended run reuses the old player, score, resources, world, pressure, construction, roster, inventory, and random state.
10. Rendering has no session epoch or committed-frame provenance.
